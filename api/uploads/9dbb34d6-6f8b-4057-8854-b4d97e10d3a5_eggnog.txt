Creamy Eggnog

INGREDIENTS
Sugar: 100g
Eggs: 80g
Cream: 200g
Milk: 500g
Nutmeg: 5g
Cinnamon: 2g

Instructions:

Whisk eggs and sugar until pale and fluffy.
Gently heat milk and cream, then temper into the egg mixture.
Stir in nutmeg and cinnamon.
Serve chilled or warm, with a dusting of additional nutmeg on top.
