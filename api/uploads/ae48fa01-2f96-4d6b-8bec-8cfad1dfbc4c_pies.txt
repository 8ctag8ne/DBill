Mince Pies

INGREDIENTS
Flour: 300g
Butter: 150g
Sugar: 50g
Cinnamon: 3g
Mincemeat: 400g

Instructions:

Rub flour and butter together until crumbly; add sugar and a pinch of cinnamon.
Press dough into pie molds and fill with mincemeat.
Cover with a pastry lid or decorative cutouts.
Bake at 200Â°C for 20-25 minutes until golden.
Serve warm with a dusting of powdered sugar.
