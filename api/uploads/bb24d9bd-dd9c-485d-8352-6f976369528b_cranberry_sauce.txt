Cranberry Sauce

INGREDIENTS
Sugar: 150g
Fresh Cranberries: 300g
Orange Zest: 5g
Water: 100g
Cinnamon: 3g

Instructions:

Combine cranberries, sugar, water, and orange zest in a saucepan.
Bring to a simmer, stirring occasionally.
Add cinnamon and cook until cranberries burst and sauce thickens (about 10 minutes).
Cool and serve with your holiday feast.
