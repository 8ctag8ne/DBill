Chocolate Yule Log

INGREDIENTS
Flour: 100g
Sugar: 120g
Butter: 50g
Cocoa Powder: 30g
Whipped Cream: 200g
Chocolate Ganache: 200g
Cinnamon: 2g

Instructions:

Whisk eggs and sugar until thick. Gently fold in flour, cocoa, and a pinch of cinnamon.
Spread onto a baking tray and bake at 190Â°C for 8-10 minutes.
Roll the sponge while warm, then unroll, fill with whipped cream, and roll again.
Cover with chocolate ganache and decorate to resemble a log.
Chill before serving.
